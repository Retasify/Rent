Warm Winter Project

by Hope Heaven Community Center

Thousands of families in underserved neighborhoods are facing the harsh winter without adequate clothing. Hope Heaven Community Center is collecting warm clothes — jackets, sweaters, scarves, and blankets — to help keep children and adults safe and comfortable during the coldest months. Your gently used winter wear can bring warmth and hope to those who need it most.
